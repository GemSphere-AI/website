Case Study

Healthcare SaaS Platform Transformation

We partnered with a leading healthcare provider to modernize their patient management system. The goal was to reduce administrative overhead and improve patient engagement through a unified digital platform.

The Challenge

The client was struggling with fragmented legacy systems, leading to data silos and slow response times. Appointments were managed manually, and patient records were difficult to access securely.

Our Solution

GemSphere AI engineered a cloud-native SaaS platform featuring:

  • Secure Patient Portal
  • AI-Powered Appointment Scheduling
  • Real-time Data Analytics
  • HIPAA Compliant Infrastructure

The Result

The new platform achieved 40% reduction in administrative tasks and a 30% increase in patient satisfaction scores within the first quarter of deployment.

Project Info

Client: HealthTech Solutions Inc.

Category: Web Development / SaaS

Duration: 6 Months

Technologies: React, Node.js, AWS, Python
